# Draft Quality Checklist

## Level 1: Safety

- No private data, tokens, or account details.
- No fabricated experience, metric, quote, or source.
- Risky factual claims are marked for checking.

## Level 2: Readability

- The article has a clear opening, middle, and ending.
- Paragraphs are short enough to scan.
- Section headings reveal progression.

## Level 3: Evidence

- Core claims have source support.
- Examples prove the point instead of decorating it.
- The article separates observation from interpretation.

## Level 4: Style

- Tone matches the style profile.
- Concrete scenes appear before abstract lessons.
- Repeated phrases and filler are removed.

## Level 5: Publishing Readiness

- Title direction is aligned with the draft.
- Summary and CTA are present.
- Image needs and fact-check items are listed.
