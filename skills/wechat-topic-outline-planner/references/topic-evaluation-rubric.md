# Topic Evaluation Rubric

Score each angle on a 100-point scale.

- Reader relevance: 25
- Evidence strength: 20
- Novelty or tension: 15
- Writeability: 15
- Cold-start clarity: 15
- Fit with author voice: 10

For each angle, provide one-sentence definition, target reader, why it is worth writing, main risk, and recommendation level.
