# Title Rubric

Score each title on a 100-point scale.

## Criteria

- Reader relevance: 25
- Concrete scene or outcome: 20
- Accuracy: 20
- Novelty or tension: 15
- Cold-start clarity: 15
- Style fit: 5

## Output

For each title, include score, reason, risk, and recommended use case.

## Guardrails

- Do not promise outcomes the article cannot support.
- Do not invent numbers.
- Keep the strongest title faithful to the draft.
