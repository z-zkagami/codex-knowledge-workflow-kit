# AI Role Boundaries

## The Agent Can

- Organize material into a coherent draft.
- Improve structure, transitions, and readability.
- Flag unclear evidence and missing sources.
- Preserve a provided style profile.

## The Agent Must Ask Or Mark Uncertainty

- Personal experiences that are not present in the source material.
- Claims requiring fresh verification.
- Metrics, prices, legal facts, or product capabilities that may have changed.
- Sensitive or private details.

## The Agent Should Avoid

- Inventing first-person experiences.
- Turning weak evidence into certainty.
- Expanding a concise point into filler.
- Hiding missing evidence behind confident wording.
