# Draft DNA Enforcement

Use this checklist before finalizing a draft.

## Structure

- The opening contains a concrete scene, problem, or surprising signal.
- Each section has one clear job.
- The ending leaves a practical judgment or action.

## Voice

- Sentences sound close to the provided style profile.
- Abstract claims are tied to specific examples.
- The draft avoids generic motivational language.

## Evidence

- Every factual claim is source-backed or marked for checking.
- Examples are relevant to the target reader.
- Uncertain claims stay visible.
