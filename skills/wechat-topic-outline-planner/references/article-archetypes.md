# Article Archetypes

## Field Test

A hands-on story about trying a tool, workflow, or method. Emphasize concrete steps, failure points, and real outcomes.

## Technical Explainer

A clear explanation of a product, mechanism, paper, or architecture. Emphasize definitions, context, and evidence.

## Method Guide

A reusable method derived from repeated practice. Emphasize steps, prerequisites, and limits.

## Opinion Analysis

A reasoned judgment about a trend or event. Emphasize evidence, alternative explanations, and practical implications.

## Signal Brief

A short interpretation of a public signal. Emphasize what changed, why it matters, and what to watch next.

Use the archetype to decide the outline, evidence style, and draft quality checks.
